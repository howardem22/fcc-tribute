Michael Crichton

Numerous works by Crichton have been adapted for film and television.

  • Born 23 October 1942 in Chicago, IL
  • Works published under the pen name "John Lange"
    • 1966: Odds On
    • 1967: Scratch One
    • 1968: Easy Go (also titled The Last Tomb)
    • 1969: Zero Cool
    • 1969: The Venom Business
    • 1970: Drug of Choice (also titled Overkill)
    • 1970: Grave Descend
    • 1972: Binary
  • Works published under the pen name "Jeffery Hudson"
    • 1968: A Case of Need
  • Works published as Michael Crichton
    • 1969: The Andromeda Strain
    • 1972: The Terminal Man
    • 1975: The Great Train Robbery
    • 1976: Eaters of the Dead (also titled The 13th Warrior)
    • 1980: Congo
    • 1987: Sphere
    • 1990: Jurassic Park
    • 1992: Rising Sun
    • 1994: Disclosure
    • 1995: The Lost World
    • 1996: Airframe
    • 1999: Timeline
    • 2002: Prey
    • 2004: State of Fear
    • 2006: Next
  • Works published posthumously after his death in 2008
    • 2009: Pirate Latitudes
    • 2011: Micro
    • 2017: Dragon Teeth
